Our Services

Comprehensive Men's Health Solutions

01

Male Infertility Treatment

Semen analysis, hormone testing, microsurgical sperm extraction (micro-TESE) and comprehensive treatment solutions

  • Complete semen quality assessment
  • Minimally invasive varicocele treatment
  • Assisted reproductive technology consultation
02

Sexual Dysfunction Therapy

Professional diagnosis and treatment of erectile dysfunction (ED), premature ejaculation, and decreased libido

  • Low-intensity shockwave therapy (Li-SWT)
  • PRP platelet-rich plasma therapy
  • Medication and lifestyle guidance
03

Prostate Disease Management

Diagnosis and treatment of prostatitis and benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH)

  • Precision imaging-guided biopsy
  • Minimally invasive surgical treatment
  • Long-term follow-up management
04

Hormone Optimization

Testosterone replacement therapy (TRT), comprehensive endocrine imbalance evaluation and treatment

  • Comprehensive hormone level testing
  • Personalized hormone replacement protocols
  • Efficacy monitoring and dosage adjustment
05

Genital Reconstructive Surgery

Circumcision, penile lengthening and augmentation, Peyronie's disease treatment

  • Minimally invasive surgical techniques
  • Rapid recovery protocols
  • Aesthetic and functional balance
06

Men's Health Screening

Comprehensive men's health evaluation, preventive screening and health management

  • Tumor marker screening
  • Cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Personalized health reports
